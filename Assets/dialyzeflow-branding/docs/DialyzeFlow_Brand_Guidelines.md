# DialyzeFlow Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

### Brand Name
**DialyzeFlow** - Written as one word with capital D and F
- Correct: DialyzeFlow
- Incorrect: Dialyze Flow, dialyzeflow, DIALYZEFLOW, Dialyze-Flow

### Tagline
**Primary:** "AI-Powered Dialysis Scheduling"
**Alternative:** "Transform Your Dialysis Unit"

## Logo Usage

### Logo Variations
1. **Full Logo** - Icon + "DialyzeFlow" text + tagline
2. **Horizontal Logo** - Icon + "DialyzeFlow" text
3. **Icon Only** - For app icons, favicons, small spaces
4. **White/Light Version** - For dark backgrounds

### Minimum Sizes
- Full Logo: Min width 200px
- Icon Only: Min 32px × 32px
- Favicon: 16px × 16px (use simplified version)

### Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "D" in DialyzeFlow around the logo.

### Don'ts
- Don't stretch or distort the logo
- Don't change the colors outside brand palette
- Don't add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
- Don't place on busy backgrounds without contrast
